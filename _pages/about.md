# Welcome to RhizoSensing

RhizoSensing is a research initiative focused on understanding how plant roots interact with soil environments under both optimal and stress conditions.

Our research combines plant physiology, root biology, rhizosphere ecology, molecular biology, and advanced sensing technologies to improve crop resilience and agricultural sustainability.

## Research Areas

### Root Biology

Understanding root growth, architecture, and environmental adaptation.

### Soil Compaction and Root Responses

Investigating how soil physical constraints influence root development and crop performance.

### Rhizosphere Biology

Exploring interactions between roots, soil architecture, and the surrounding environment.

### Soil Stresses

Studying plant adaptation mechanisms under heterogeneous soil and other abiotic stresses.

### Sensing and Phenotyping

Developing innovative tools to monitor root and soil processes in real time.
