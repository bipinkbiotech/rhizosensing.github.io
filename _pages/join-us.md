# Join Us

RhizoSensing welcomes enquiries from motivated students and researchers interested in:

- Root biology
- Soil compaction
- Rhizosphere interactions
- Plant stress biology
- Root phenotyping
- Soil sensing technologies

## PhD Opportunities

Prospective PhD students are encouraged to contact Dr Bipin Pandey with:

- CV
- Research interests
- Academic transcripts

## Postdoctoral Researchers

We welcome enquiries from researchers interested in:

- Fellowship applications (EMBO, MSCA-PF, RS-URF, HFSP etc)
- Collaborative projects
- Research partnerships

## Visiting Researchers

Opportunities may be available for visiting scholars and international collaborators.

## Contact
